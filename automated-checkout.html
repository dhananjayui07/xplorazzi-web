How does it work?

Take Picture

Once items are kept on the Kiosk platform, camera captures the images and combine a interface request to send for SKU detection engine. These are basic cameras and multiple in numbers.

Image Recognition

SKU recognition engine(this can be hosted in premise/cloud) receives the images to extract SKU information from the image. SKU extraction takes less than 1.5 Sec to bring out SKU level details.

Billing

Once system gathers the SKU level details.Kiosk fetches the further details from inventory which helps generate bills for the consumers. Consumers make the payment and inventory gets updated.
