How does it work?

Take Picture

Our xtract app manages tasks for ground staff and let them capture images with proper guidence so that quality images are captured. Shelf camera also can be installed to capture the images and upload to xtract server.

Image Recognition

On cloud SKU recognition engine receives the images to extract SKU information from the image. Once images are processed, various KPIs are calculated as below -
OOS
SOS
Compliance score
Empty Space
Competitor presence

Corrective Actions

Based on the KPIs, users also recives actionable insights to perform and take corrective action to make planogram compliance score 100%.
