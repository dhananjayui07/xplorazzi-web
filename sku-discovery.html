SKU Discovery

A shelf scanner app which can be used by consumers while visiting the retail store and find the required items in real-time by just scanning the shelf. While they find the SKUs on the shelf by the App, they can also look at additional details about the SKUs and understand the offering and promotions.

How does it work?

Scan QR

Consumers viists retail store and find a QR code on the brands shelf. While consumers scans the QR, a web App open with list of items. And select one/multiple items to search.

Shelf Scan

Once consumers selects the items from the list, web App opens the camera which start looking for the selected items. Consumers have to just move from one edge to other in the aisle. Once the items are found, it gets highlighted.

More Information

Once the items are highlighted during the scan, consumers can tap and see more details about the items. And also explores promotion and different offer related to the items.
